Positioning the pi

You want to position your camera close to a power source and near an ethernet port (unless you plan to use wifi).

The camera should be placed in a position where people are likely to be looking roughly towards the camera when entering a team space.

The area the camera watches should be well lit and have as higher contrast with people as possible.

The camera should be completely horizontal with the cable either vertically up or down.

There are two main techniques to connecting your system to the internet:

  1. Setting up with wifi - easier to position but slower reaction speeds and harder to setup.
  2. Setting up with Ethernet - harder to position.

Using Ethernet

Once you have powered on and positioned the Raspberry Pi you should connect an ethernet cable from your network into the Raspberry Pi. After doing that visit the IP Panel where your local Web Panel IP should become visible - when you are in the same network.
